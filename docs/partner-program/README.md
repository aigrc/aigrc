## Quick Start

### For New Partners

1. **Enroll** in the Partner Program via [partners.aigos.io](https://partners.aigos.io)
2. **Complete** Foundation modules (Week 1)
3. **Pass** Associate Certification exam
4. **Begin** assisted implementations

### For Experienced Partners

1. **Review** updated training materials
2. **Complete** annual recertification requirements
3. **Access** advanced resources in Partner Portal

## Certification Paths

```
Associate (3 weeks) → Professional (6 weeks) → Expert (8 weeks) → Master (12 weeks)
```

| Level | Enables | Requirements |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| **Associate** | Discovery, assisted implementations | 20 hrs training, 4 labs, exam (70%) |
| **Professional** | Independent implementations, training delivery | 40 hrs training, capstone, exam (80%) |
| **Expert** | Agentic governance, advanced integrations | 60 hrs training, 3 implementations, exam (85%) |
| **Master** | Enterprise architecture, train-the-trainer | 80 hrs training, 5 implementations, board review |

## Learning Tracks

### Business Track
For GRC consultants, compliance officers, and risk managers.
- Focuses on governance concepts, compliance mapping, audit support
- 25-30 hours of content

### Technical Track
For solution architects, DevOps engineers, and developers.
- Focuses on platform deployment, integrations, custom detection
- 35-40 hours of content

### Delivery Track
For project managers, customer success managers, and implementation leads.
- Focuses on methodology, change management, customer success
- 20-25 hours of content

## Implementation Framework

```
DISCOVER (2-3 wks) → DESIGN (3-4 wks) → BUILD (4-6 wks) → ENABLE (3-4 wks) → SUSTAIN
```

Total typical engagement: **10-16 weeks**
