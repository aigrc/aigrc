## 1. Associate Certification

### Target Audience
- New partner consultants
- Pre-sales engineers
- Junior GRC practitioners
- Technical support staff

### Prerequisites
- GRC domain knowledge (1+ years)
- Basic technical literacy
- Partner organization enrollment

### Curriculum (3 weeks / 20 hours)

| Week | Focus | Hours | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Foundations | 8 hrs | Quiz 1 passed |
| 2 | Platform Skills | 8 hrs | Labs 1-4 completed |
| 3 | Customer Engagement | 4 hrs | Mock discovery completed |

### Required Modules

**Week 1: Foundations**
- Module F1: Why AI Governance Matters (2 hrs)
- Module F2: AIGOS Platform Overview (2 hrs)
- Module F3: Risk Classification Framework (2 hrs)
- Module F4: Regulatory Landscape (2 hrs)

**Week 2: Platform Skills**
- Lab 1: First AI Discovery Scan (1 hr)
- Lab 2: Creating Asset Cards (1.5 hrs)
- Lab 3: Running Compliance Checks (1.5 hrs)
- Lab 4: Report Generation (1 hr)
- Self-study: CLI basics (3 hrs)

**Week 3: Customer Engagement**
- Workshop: Discovery facilitation (2 hrs)
- Role-play: Customer presentation (2 hrs)

### Assessment

**Written Exam:**
- Format: 60 multiple choice questions
- Duration: 90 minutes
- Passing score: 70%
- Attempts: 3 (7-day wait between)

**Practical Assessment:**
- Complete Labs 1-4 with validation
- Pass all lab checkpoints

### Sample Exam Questions

```
Q1: Under EU AI Act, which risk category requires conformity assessment
    before market placement?
    A) Minimal risk
    B) Limited risk
    C) High risk ✓
    D) All categories

Q2: What is the primary purpose of an AIGOS Asset Card?
    A) Store machine learning model weights
    B) Document AI system governance metadata ✓
    C) Execute AI inference requests
    D) Manage user authentication

Q3: Which command generates a compliance report?
    A) aigrc scan
    B) aigrc check
    C) aigrc report ✓
    D) aigrc audit
```

### Certification Badge

Upon passing, you receive:
- Digital badge for LinkedIn/email signature
- Listing in Partner Directory (Associate tier)
- Access to Associate resources in Partner Portal

## 2. Professional Certification

### Target Audience
- Senior consultants
- Implementation leads
- Technical architects (foundational)
- Customer success managers

### Prerequisites
- Associate Certification (valid)
- 2+ years GRC or technical experience
- Completed at least 1 assisted implementation

### Curriculum (6 weeks / 40 hours)

| Week | Focus | Hours | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1-2 | Track Specialization | 16 hrs | Track modules completed |
| 3-4 | Advanced Skills | 12 hrs | Labs 5-8 completed |
| 5 | Implementation Methodology | 8 hrs | Project plan created |
| 6 | Capstone | 4 hrs | Capstone project delivered |

### Track Options (Choose One)

**Business Track:**
- Module B1: Compliance Framework Deep Dive (4 hrs)
- Module B2: Policy Architecture Design (4 hrs)
- Module B3: Audit Evidence Management (4 hrs)
- Module B4: Executive Communication (4 hrs)
- Lab 5: Dashboard Operations (1.5 hrs)
- Lab 6: Discovery Workshop Simulation (2 hrs)

**Technical Track:**
- Module T1: CLI Mastery (3 hrs)
- Module T2: Integration Patterns (4 hrs)
- Module T3: Policy Engine Configuration (3 hrs)
- Module T4: Detection Rule Development (4 hrs)
- Lab 7: CI/CD Integration Setup (2 hrs)
- Lab 8: Policy Configuration (1.5 hrs)

**Delivery Track:**
- Module D1: Implementation Methodology (4 hrs)
- Module D2: Change Management (4 hrs)
- Module D3: Training Program Design (4 hrs)
- Module D4: Customer Success Metrics (4 hrs)
- Lab: Project Planning Exercise (1.5 hrs)
- Lab: Training Delivery Simulation (2 hrs)

### Assessment

**Written Exam:**
- Format: 50 multiple choice + 3 scenario questions
- Duration: 120 minutes
- Passing score: 80%

**Practical Assessment:**
- Complete all track labs with validation
- Submit capstone project
- Peer review evaluation

**Capstone Project:**

*Business Track:*
- Develop mock customer proposal
- Include gap analysis, timeline, budget
- Present to review panel (30 min)

*Technical Track:*
- Implement end-to-end integration in sandbox
- Document architecture decisions
- Demo to review panel (30 min)

*Delivery Track:*
- Create customer training curriculum
- Deliver sample training session
- Present success measurement plan (30 min)

### Certification Badge

Upon passing, you receive:
- Professional Certified Partner badge
- Independent implementation authorization
- Customer training delivery rights (assisted)
- Priority support access

## Exam Scheduling

### Booking Process
1. Log into Partner Portal
2. Navigate to Certifications > Schedule Exam
3. Select certification level
4. Choose date/time (available slots shown)
5. Confirm proctoring requirements
6. Complete payment (if applicable)

### Exam Policies

**Proctoring:**
- All exams are online proctored
- Camera and microphone required
- Valid ID for identity verification
- Quiet, private testing environment

**Rescheduling:**
- > 48 hours notice: Free reschedule
- 24-48 hours notice: $50 fee
- < 24 hours notice: Forfeit exam attempt

**Results:**
- Immediate results for written exams
- 5 business days for practical assessments
- 10 business days for capstone reviews
